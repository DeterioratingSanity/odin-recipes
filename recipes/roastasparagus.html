Roasted
Asparagus

Description

A simple preparation for a side-dish of asparagus, which serves well to accompany many dishes.

This method is especially worthwhile when other dishes may already occupy the stovetop, but the oven is comparatively unused. Alternatively, cook time and temperature may be adjusted to accomodate other dishes one wishes to prepare concurrently in the oven.

Ingredients

  • One bunch asparagus
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic powder
  • Lemon juice

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425F
  2. Lay parchment paper on a baking sheet
  3. Chop asparagus into 3-inch sections, lay out on paper
  4. Top with cheese, garlic powder, and lemon juice
  5. Place in oven, cook for 12-15 minutes
  6. Remove and serve
